Who is Jack Buckingham?

 Jack Buckingham is a graphic designer with a particular interest in production design and logo design. He enjoys experimenting with various other mediums of art throughout the years including costume and prop design, set design, drawing, painting, 3D design, and so much more. He is eager and ready to face the task before him and use the many skills he’s learned to push him further into his graphic design career and become a successful artist. He has a particular interest in graphic design because he finds it enjoyable working with the many tools and effects available and thinks of it as the future of art. Digital art mediums such as adobe and blender are the current trend in art, and as technology develops and improves, Jack Buckingham will as well.
